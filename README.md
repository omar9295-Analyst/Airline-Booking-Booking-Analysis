# Airline Booking Data Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
A comprehensive data analysis project focused on evaluating airline passenger booking behaviors, channel performance, and geographical trends. Built entirely in Excel with a custom-designed UI, this dashboard provides actionable insights into conversion rates, trip preferences, and high-demand routes to optimize airline operations and marketing strategies.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

1- * **Data Analysis & Visualization:** Microsoft Excel (Advanced Formulas, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts)..

2- * **Data Cleaning & Transformation:** Power Query.

3- * **UI/UX Design:** Canva (used for designing the sleek, modern background and custom navigation elements).

## 📊 Core KPIs

1- * **Total Passengers:** 80K

2- * **Potential Customers:** 4M

3- * **Average Flight Duration:** 7.5 Hours

4- * **Completed Bookings:** 15% (vs. 85% Incomplete)

5- * **Most Wanted Route:** AKLKUL (Auckland to Kuala Lumpur)

6- * **Most Wanted Day:** Monday

7- * **Peak Booking Hour:** 8:00

## 💡 Key Business Insights

1- * **Channel Performance:** The Internet is the dominant sales channel, accounting for 89% of passengers and driving 92% of all completed bookings. It also boasts a higher conversion rate (18.13%) compared to Mobile (13.30%).

2- * **Geographical Trends:** Australia is the highest origin for bookings and leads in requesting extra services (27,747 requests). However, Malaysia is the most effective market for finalizing transactions, leading the "Completed Booking" metric (2,468 completions).

3- * **Trip Type Preferences:** "RoundTrip" is overwhelmingly the most popular choice, accounting for 78,774 passengers and 7,453 completed bookings.

4- * **Behavioral Metrics:** While RoundTrips dominate volume, "CircleTrip" passengers book the furthest in advance (Average Lead Time: 95.3 days) and have the longest average stay (25.2 days).

5- * **Route Dynamics:** The AKLKUL route sees its highest passenger traffic on Sundays (798 passengers) and Wednesdays (709 passengers).

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations

1- **Optimize Mobile Experience:** With Mobile accounting for only 11% of passengers and a lower conversion rate (13.30%), a technical and UX review of the mobile booking app/flow is highly recommended to capture lost revenue.

2- **Investigate Drop-off Rates:** An 85% incomplete booking rate indicates a significant bottleneck in the user journey or pricing strategy. Retargeting campaigns and streamlined checkout processes are essential to improve conversions.

3-  **Targeted Regional Marketing:** Leverage the high engagement in Australia to upsell premium services, while studying the Malaysian market's checkout behavior to replicate its high completion success rate globally.
